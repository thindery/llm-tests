## Brand Concept: Agent Paige

Paige works as an AI assistant persona - warm, approachable, professional. She helps tradespeople build websites without the tech headaches. Think of her as the friendly office assistant who just happens to be really good at web design.

**Key Traits:**
- Female name = approachable, trustworthy
- Sounds human, not robotic
- Easy to pronounce and spell
- Memorable for radio/word-of-mouth referrals
- Fits the "helper" archetype tradespeople want
